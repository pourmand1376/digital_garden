[Meeting notes template](https://www.atlassian.com/software/confluence/templates/meeting-notes)


They typically contain the following information:
- Meeting date and time 
- Participants and contact details
- Meeting goals
- Discussion topics
- Action items
- Decisions
- Next meeting date and time

### Tips
- Promptly Share notes with coworkers
- Encourage others to participate
