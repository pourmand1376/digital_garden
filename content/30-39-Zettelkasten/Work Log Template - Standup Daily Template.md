### Why this works:
- **The Traffic Light System:** Adding a color-coded status at the top allows managers or teammates to see instantly who needs help without reading every bullet point.
- **Outcome over Activity:** It encourages people to list what they _achieved_ rather than just a list of meetings they attended.
- **The "Parking Lot":** It keeps the actual standup meeting short by pushing deep-dive technical discussions to the end.
### Pro-Tips for the Team:
- **Keep it under 100 words:** If it’s longer than that, it’s a report, not a log.
- **Update before the meeting:** If you're doing a live sync, having the log written beforehand keeps the conversation crisp.
- **Use @mentions:** In digital logs, tagging people directly in the "Blockers" section ensures they see the request immediately.
