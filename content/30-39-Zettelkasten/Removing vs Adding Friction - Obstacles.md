If you want to have significant changes in your life, there are basically two ways;; Push The Changes, Add or Remove Friction

Pushing the changes is nice. But It doesn't work. Most of the time `motivaton` alone doesn't work. That's why we have motivational videos don't work. 

You want to do something to **remove the friction to achieve the goal**. Or **add a friction** to the things that **prevent** you from doing it.
