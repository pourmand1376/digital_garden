There are multiple reasons on why we should consume each information multiple times. 

Even Matt says that we need to see his videos at least three times. 

There are reasons for that:
- Each time we are a different person. We change as time passes. 
- We consume information with different problems in mind. So, we see only the parts that we are interested in. 

> [!quote]
> A great book is a book that every time you read it, it opens doors that you haven't thought about.
