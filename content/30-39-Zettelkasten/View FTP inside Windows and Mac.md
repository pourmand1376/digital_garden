Chrome has removed internal ftp handling. But you can use these internal tools inside mac and windows to see ftp. 

| **Operating System** | **Steps to Connect**                                                                                                    |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Windows 10/11**    | Open **File Explorer**, type your address (e.g., `ftp://yourserver.com`) into the top address bar, and press **Enter**. |
| **macOS**            | Open **Finder**, click **Go** in the top menu, select **Connect to Server...**, and enter your FTP address.             |
