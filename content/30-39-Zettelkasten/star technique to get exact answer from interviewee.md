Gemini 3. 

برای پاسخ دادن به این سوالات، از فرمول **STAR** استفاده کن تا پاسخی منسجم و حرفه‌ای داشته باشی:

1. **S**ituation (موقعیت): خیلی کوتاه بستر داستان را تعریف کن.
2. **T**ask (وظیفه): بگو هدف یا وظیفه دقیق تو در آن لحظه چه بود.
3. **A**ction (اقدام): توضیح بده که **دقیقاً تو** چه کارهایی انجام دادی (تمرکز روی خودت باشد، نه تیم).
4. **R**esult (نتیجه): در نهایت چه شد؟ (اگر عدد و رقم داری، اینجا بگو).

| **Component** | **Description**                                                      | **Goal**                                             |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **S**ituation | Set the scene. Give the context of what was happening.               | Provide just enough background (1-2 sentences).      |
| **T**ask      | Explain the challenge or the specific goal you were working toward.  | Define the problem you needed to solve.              |
| **A**ction    | **The most important part.** Detail the specific steps **you** took. | Show _how_ you think and work. Use "I," not "we."    |
| **R**esult    | Share the outcome. What happened? What did you achieve or learn?     | Use data, numbers, or positive feedback if possible. |

### A Quick Example

**Question:** _"Tell me about a time you handled a difficult client."_
- **Situation:** "At my last job, a long-term client was angry because a software update caused a bug in their reporting system."
- **Task:** "My goal was to de-escalate the situation and ensure the bug was fixed before their end-of-month deadline."
- **Action:** "I scheduled a call to listen to their concerns without interrupting. Then, I prioritized the ticket with our dev team and provided the client with twice-daily status updates so they never felt in the dark."
- **Result:** "We fixed the bug 48 hours early. The client was so impressed with the communication that they renewed their contract for another year."
