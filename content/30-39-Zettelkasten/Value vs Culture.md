- از هم تشکر کنن

Gemini 3.

> If you think of a society as a tree, **values** are the invisible root system, and **culture** is the visible fruit, leaves, and bark.

Sociologists often use the iceberg analogy to explain this exact relationship:

- **Surface Culture (The "Show"):** This is what you see, hear, and touch. It’s the food, holidays, language, dress, and music.
- **Deep Culture (The Values):** This is the 90% underwater. It’s the unspoken rules about eye contact, the definition of "success," how we treat the elderly, and our concepts of time.
