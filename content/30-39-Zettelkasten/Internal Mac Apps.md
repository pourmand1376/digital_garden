### [Productivity](Index%20-%20Productivity.md) Tools
- **Preview:** View, sign, and edit PDFs or quickly remove backgrounds from images.
- **Shortcuts:** Create custom automations to perform multi-step tasks with a single click.
- **Activity Monitor:** Identify and force-quit apps that are hogging your CPU, RAM, or battery.
- **Spotlight (`Cmd+Space`):** Instantly find files, launch apps, perform calculations, and convert units.
### Utility Tools
- **Audio MIDI Setup:** Configure advanced audio hardware or play sound through two sets of speakers at once.
- **Digital Color Meter:** Hover over any pixel on your screen to see its exact color code for design work.
- **Disk Utility:** Format external drives, repair disk errors, and create encrypted folders for sensitive files.
- **Screenshot Utility (`Cmd+Shift+5`):** Capture or record your screen with advanced options like timers and specific save locations.
### Maintenance & Hidden Tools
- **Wireless Diagnostics:** Analyze your Wi-Fi connection to find interference and optimize your signal.
- **Keychain Access:** View and manage all your saved passwords, Wi-Fi keys, and secure notes.
- **Terminal:** Control the entire operating system through text commands for tasks the visual interface can't do.
- **Screen Sharing:** Remotely view and control another Mac on your network or via an Apple ID.
