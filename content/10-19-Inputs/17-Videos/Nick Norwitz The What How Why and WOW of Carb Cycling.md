### How to break a fast
- Don't eat a lot when breaking a fast. Start light. 
- Avoid nuts and seeds and fiber and vegetables in first hours
- Eat three boiled hard eggs or bone broth or small full fat greek yogurt
- Wait some hours before having a full meal

> [!important]
> If you take 3+ days of fasting or long-term ketogenic, be extra careful about Refeeding Syndrome. Don't start with carbs. Don't eat quickly. 

- [Refeeding Syndrome: Symptoms, Treatment & Risk Factors](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/diseases/23228-refeeding-syndrome)

### How to Carb Cycle
1. Daily Carb Cycling: Short eating time (less than 6 hours)
2. Weekly Carb Cycling: 1 Carb day per week
3. Seasonal / Long Term Carb Cycling: Do it on vacations 

### Do you need to Carb Cycle?
No. We need Growth and renewal phases. For stimulating growth hormone, we would need insulin spikes. Some proteins also increase insulin a lot. 

So, you wouldn't need carbs for that growth. 

Specially Whey protein. 

So, you can get out of ketosis from eating protein but since you are not filling up your glycogen stores, you would be in ketosis quickly again.
